At Los Angeles Senior Services we
are more than just a healthcare
provider we are a Trusted Partner
in the Well-Being of Seniors in the
Los Angeles Community.

Our mission is to build
Life-long Relationships with those
who can Benefit from High-Quality
Healthcare Programs covered by
Medicare & Medi-Cal.

We believe every Senior deserves
Compassionate, Personalized Care that
enhances their Quality of Life while
providing Peace of Mind to their
Families.

Whether it’s Skilled Nursing, Therapy,
Personal Care, or Chronic Disease
Management, our dedicated
team ensures that every individual
receives the support they need—right in
the comfort of their home.

By guiding seniors and their loved ones
through accessible healthcare solutions,
we help them maintain their
independence, dignity, and health for
years to come.

At LASS, we don’t just provide care—
we create lasting connections that make a difference.

Let’s build a healthier, happier future
together.
