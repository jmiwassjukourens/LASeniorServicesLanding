LOS ANGELES SENIOR SERVICES

Who Are We?

Los Angeles Senior Services is a Community Agency that helps Seniors all over Los Angeles County & Orange County access to Quality Health Care Services covered by Medical & Medicare.

Our Mission is to Improve Seniors Life-Quality by engaging them in tailored Health Care Programs according to their needs.

We build Life-Long Relationships were not only Health Care Needs are taking care off, but also Social, Recreational & Spiritual.

Our Line is Open 24 hs to Help Seniors & Families Find the Health Care Service they need.
